TimeBank Planner and Scheduling

The name of the app suggests the unique time management principle by calling it a "time bank", and also lets the user know that the primary functions are "planning" and "scheduling"

Purpose: for people who value their time

The app provides time management tools that cater to the needs of both professional and personal use to help reduce time wasters, and plan schedules critically and effectively.

Scenarios: Use Case Questions

How much time do I really have to get this done?

Where is all my time going?! I never have any time!
